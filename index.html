Sudoku History
Sudoku originated in the 18th century, evolving into the modern logic puzzle we know today. It became widely popular through Japanese publications in the late 20th century and remains a favorite mental challenge worldwide.

HOW TO PLAY?

Sudoku is a logic-based puzzle where you fill a 9×9 grid with numbers 1–9. Each row, column, and 3×3 subgrid must contain each number only once.

When stuck, scan the entire grid for "single candidates" (cells that can only contain one number) and look for "naked pairs." These strategies help you eliminate numbers in other cells and progress!
